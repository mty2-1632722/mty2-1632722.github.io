Summit Lake

A short hike along the Mount Rainier Area

Beautiful Hike for the Summer

Looking to start hiking?

This is a great hike for beginners or those of all ages looking to explore the great outdoors! Along the 6 mile trail, you will find various types of wildlife along the way such as deers, elk, bear, and a snake or two. Be careful to stay with the hiking party, and listen as the leader of our hike tells you what to do when you see these animals. Beyond the animals, there are various meadows, lakes, and trees.

Don't forget to bring you camera, and a couple of snacks. Usually, this hike will take the group 3 hours to complete from start to finish, stopping many times along the way to hydrate, and document the beautiful scenery. We will begin the hike at the Carbon River Trailhead, and allow you to move at your own pace, but advise that you never leave the trail.

If this sounds like your kind of hike, call our offices to sign up for the next hiking trip!
