Jade Lake

A weekend trip in the Snoqualmie Region

A Hike for the Dedicated

Take a longer trip, going off trail

This is a great hike for the more experienced hiker. It runs roughly 21 miles, and goes off the trial at times to reach the beautiful bright blue lake we call Jade Lake. Along the way we will pass Marmot Lake, and build a campsite at Pea Soup Lake. Unlike most hikes, this one will cover the span of 3 days. Although it could be taken in a shorter time, we want to allow you all to experience the spectacular views.

Make sure to bring bug spray, and lots of water as this trip can be very demanding. Since we only offer these trips during the heat of the summer, the bugs will be out in full force.

If this sounds like your kind of hike, call our offices to sign up for the next hiking trip! Prior to the hike, we will require that you sign a waiver form, which states that we are not responsible for any injuries along the way.
